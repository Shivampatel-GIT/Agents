# 🧠 Human-in-the-Loop Expense Approval (LangGraph)

> AI handles routine work. Humans stay in control of critical decisions.

---

## 🚩 Problem

Finance teams waste hours reviewing expenses manually:
- Small expenses → repetitive ✅  
- Large expenses → risky ⚠️  
- Delays → 3–5 days ⏳  

---

## 💡 Solution 

Use **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)** with LangGraph:
- 🤖 AI auto-approves small expenses  
- 🧑‍💼 Humans review high-value ones  
- ⚡ Faster + safer decisions  
  
---
## 📈 Impact

- ⏱️ Approval time: **days → minutes**  
- 🧾 70% expenses auto-approved  
- 🛡️ High-value decisions remain human-controlled  
- 📉 Finance workload drastically reduced  

---
## 🌍 Use Cases

- 🛑 Content moderation  
- 📄 Legal document review  
- 🧑‍⚕️ Medical workflows  
- 💼 Hiring pipelines  
- 📧 Marketing approvals
